Our philosophy

Tacking Capital exists to profitably help build a resilient, decentralized and anonymous transaction ecosystem for ESG-excluded and financially censored industries and provide prospects in these industries with the financing they need.

Our mission

To provide industry-leading rates of return for our investors; by
  • Facilitating individual freedom by investing in high-growth ventures in ESG-excluded industries and privacy enhanced cryptocurrencies; while
  • Facilitating the adoption of privacy-enhanced cryptocurrencies

Who are we?

We are a small, dedicated team of young, experienced, motivated, liberty-oriented, interdisciplinary professionals with a no-bullshit, to-the-point attitude.
Tacking Capital is advised by several established industry experts from key underserved industries, venture capital and decentralized finance and privacy tech firms with over a century of combined global and regional experience.
